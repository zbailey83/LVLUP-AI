## üìö PROMPT LIBRARY BEST PRACTICES

### General Tips:

**1. Be Specific**
‚ùå "Write a social media post"
‚úÖ "Write a 280-character LinkedIn post about [TOPIC] targeting [AUDIENCE] with a conversational tone and a question at the end"

**2. Provide Context**
Always include:
- Who you are/what you do
- Who your audience is
- What success looks like
- Any constraints or requirements

If you have an example of what you want, include it. AI learns patterns better from examples.

If output is 70% there, use refinement prompts instead of starting over.

**5. Save Your Winners**
When a prompt works great, save it with your customizations for reuse.
