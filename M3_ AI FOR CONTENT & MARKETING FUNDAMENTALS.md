## **2\. DETAILED WRITTEN GUIDE**

### **Introduction: Consistency is the Killer**

The \#1 reason businesses fail at content marketing isn't a lack of creativity; it's a lack of **consistency**. You post three times in a "motivated" week, then ghost your audience for a month when business gets busy.

AI solves the consistency problem. But most people use it wrong. They ask ChatGPT to "Write me 10 tweets about real estate," and they get garbage that sounds like a robot.

In this module, we are building a **Content Supply Chain**. We will not ask the AI to "be creative" from scratch. We will use it to multiply your best ideas, clone your unique voice, and format everything for every platform instantly.

### **Prerequisites**

* **Your "Source Material":** Have 3-5 examples of your *best* previous content (emails, blogs, or transcripts of videos) ready. We need this to train the AI on your voice.  
* **A Content Calendar:** A simple Google Sheet or Notion Board with columns for Date, Platform, Topic, and Status.

---

### **Step 1: The Brand Voice Analyzer**

Before we write a single post, we must define **how** you sound. If you skip this, your content will sound like every other AI-generated account.

**The Strategy:** We will feed your past content into the AI and ask it to reverse-engineer your style.

1. **Gather Data:** Copy the text from your 3 best-performing posts/emails.  
2. **Run the Analysis:** Use *Prompt 1 (The Voice Decoder)* below.  
3. **Save the Output:** The AI will give you a "Style Guide" paragraph describing your tone (e.g., "Direct, witty, uses short sentences, avoids jargon"). **Save this.** You will paste this into the "Context" section of every future content prompt.

### **Step 2: The "Content Pillar" Strategy**

Don't just "post stuff." Professional accounts rotate between 3-4 core themes (Pillars).

* *Example (Fitness Coach):* Pillar 1: Nutrition Tips. Pillar 2: Workout Mistakes. Pillar 3: Client Success Stories.

**The Workflow:**

1. **Define your Pillars:** Pick your top 3\.  
2. **The "Ideation Avalanche":** Use *Prompt 2 (The Idea Matrix)*. Ask the AI to generate 10 specific, controversial, or educational sub-topics for *each* pillar.  
3. **Select:** Pick the best 10 ideas total. You don't need infinite ideas; you need 10 good ones that we will repurpose.

### **Step 3: The Repurposing Matrix (The Multiplier)**

This is where you save 20 hours a week. We are going to take **one** core idea and turn it into **five** assets.

**The "Waterfall" Method:**

Always start with the "Rich" format (Long-form), then cut it down.

* **Input:** A rough script, a brain dump, or a blog post draft about "Topic A."  
* **Output 1:** A YouTube Video Script (Hook, Body, CTA).  
* **Output 2:** A LinkedIn Text Post (Broetry style).  
* **Output 3:** A Twitter/X Thread (Punchy, thread-style).  
* **Output 4:** An Instagram Caption (Casual \+ Hashtags).  
* **Output 5:** A Newsletter blurb (Teaser).

You execute this using *Prompt 3 (The Repurposing Engine)*. You paste your rough thought once, and the AI formats it for all five platforms simultaneously.

### **Step 4: Visuals (Brief Overview)**

Text is only half the battle. You need scroll-stopping visuals.

* **Tools:** Midjourney (Advanced) or DALL-E 3 (Inside ChatGPT Plus).  
* **Strategy:** Don't ask for "A picture of a computer." Ask for specific styles.  
  * *Prompt:* "Create a flat-vector illustration style image of a chaotic office desk, using my brand colors (Orange and Blue), minimal background, aspect ratio 16:9."  
* **Consistency:** Once you find a prompt style you like, use the same style keywords for *every* image to build brand recognition.

### **Step 5: Batch Production Workflow (2-Hour Sprint)**

Here is how you actually do this in real life:

* **0:00 \- 0:20:** **Ideation.** Run the "Idea Matrix" prompt. Pick your 4 topics for the week.  
* **0:20 \- 0:40:** **Voice Calibration.** Paste your "Style Guide" into a fresh chat.  
* **0:40 \- 1:20:** **Drafting.** Feed the 4 topics into the "Repurposing Engine." You now have drafts for LinkedIn, Twitter, and IG for the whole week.  
* **1:20 \- 1:40:** **Review & Edit.** (Crucial\!) Spend 5 minutes per post injecting your human soul—add a personal story, fix a weird phrase, or add a specific client name.  
* **1:40 \- 2:00:** **Visuals.** Generate the images or headlines.

---

### **Optimization**

* **The "Trend Jacking" Method:** If big news hits your industry, paste the news article into ChatGPT and ask: *"Summarize this and give me 3 distinct 'Hot Takes' on why this matters for \[MY AUDIENCE\]. Formatting: LinkedIn Post."*  
* **Hashtag Optimization:** Don't guess hashtags. Ask the AI: *"List 15 relevant, high-traffic but low-competition hashtags for this post."*

### **Troubleshooting**

* **Issue:** *The content sounds repetitive.*  
  * **Fix:** Your "Pillars" are too similar. Force the AI to vary the "Angle." Ask for one post that is a "Rant," one that is a "How-To," and one that is a "Story."  
* **Issue:** *The hooks are boring.*  
  * **Fix:** Use a specific "Hook prompt": *"Rewrite the first sentence of these posts. Make them click-baity, open loops, or counter-intuitive statements."*

## **3\. KEY PROMPTS (Copy & Paste)**

**Prompt 1: The Voice Decoder (Run this ONCE to get your Style Guide)**

"I am going to paste 3 examples of my best writing below. Please analyze them for:

1. Tone and Voice (Adjectives).  
2. Sentence Structure (Length, rhythm).  
3. Vocabulary (Simple vs. Complex, Jargon vs. Plain English).  
4. formatting quirks (Emoji use, spacing).

Create a 'Style Guide' summary that I can paste into future prompts to teach an AI to write exactly like me.

\[PASTE EXAMPLES\]"

**Prompt 2: The Idea Matrix (The "Avalanche")**

"I need content ideas for my business, \[BUSINESS NAME\]. My target audience is \[AUDIENCE\]. My 3 Content Pillars are: \[PILLAR 1\], \[PILLAR 2\], \[PILLAR 3\].

Generate a table with 10 content ideas (rows).

Columns:

1. Topic Title.  
2. The 'Hook' (First sentence).  
3. Which Pillar it belongs to.  
4. The format (Story, Listicle, or Contrarian View).  
   Make them unique and high-value, not generic advice."

**Prompt 3: The Repurposing Engine (The "One-to-Many")**

"Context: You are my Social Media Manager. My Style Guide is: \[PASTE STYLE GUIDE SUMMARY\].

Task: I am providing a rough draft/transcript below on the topic of \[TOPIC\]. Repurpose this into the following 3 assets:

1. **LinkedIn Post:** Professional, spaced out sentences, 'Broetry' style, strong hook, Call to Action at the end.  
2. **Twitter Thread:** 5 connected tweets. Punchy, short, no fluff.  
3. **Instagram Caption:** Casual, conversational, include 3 line breaks before hashtags.

Source Text: \[PASTE TEXT\]"

**Prompt 4: The Visual Prompt Generator**

"I need an image for the LinkedIn post above. Write a detailed prompt for DALL-E 3\.

Description: A metaphorical representation of \[TOPIC\].

Style: Minimalist 3D render, isometric view, \[BRAND COLORS\] color palette, high definition, white background. No text in the image."
